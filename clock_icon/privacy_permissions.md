Title: Privacy Policy — Toolbar World Clock (Clock Icon)

 Last updated: 2026-03-22

1. Overview
    Toolbar World Clock (“the Extension”) is an offline toolbar clock that displays the current time for a selected time zone (including UTC).
2. Data We Collect
    The Extension does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data.
3. Data Stored on Your Device
    The Extension stores your settings locally using Chrome’s storage (e.g., selected time zone, 12/24-hour format, badge display options, and color flash preference). This data stays on your device and is not sent to any server.
4. Network Access
    The Extension does not communicate with external servers. No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs are used.
5. Data Sharing
    We do not share data with third parties because no data is collected or transmitted.
6. Permissions Explanation
- storage: Save your preferences locally on your device.
- alarms: Schedule periodic updates to refresh the toolbar icon time display.
