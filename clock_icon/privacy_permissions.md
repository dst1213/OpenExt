- 12/24小时制格式
- 徽章时间显示选项
- 整点半点闪烁提醒设置

**所有数据仅保存在您的本地设备上，不会发送到任何服务器。**

## 4. 网络访问

本扩展**不与外部服务器通信**，不使用任何分析、追踪或广告 SDK。

## 5. 数据共享

**我们不会与第三方共享数据**，因为本扩展不收集或传输任何数据。

# Privacy Policy — Feishu Clock Assistant

**Last updated: 2026-04-19**

## 1. Overview

Feishu Clock Assistant ("the Extension") is a browser toolbar clock extension that supports multiple timezone switching and personalized display settings.

## 2. Data Collection

**The Extension does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data.**

## 3. Local Data Storage

The Extension uses Chrome's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`) to save the following settings:

- Selected timezone (e.g., Asia/Shanghai)
- Display language preference
- 12/24-hour format
- Badge time display options
- Hour/half-hour flash reminder settings

**All data is stored locally on your device and is not sent to any server.**

## 4. Network Access

The Extension **does not communicate with external servers** and does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

## 5. Data Sharing

**We do not share data with third parties** because the Extension does not collect or transmit any data.

## 6. Permissions Explanation

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `storage` | Save your preferences locally on your device |
| `alarms` | Schedule periodic updates to refresh the toolbar icon time display |

## 7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact: anyask.service@gmail.com
